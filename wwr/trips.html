Rafting Trips Made for Everyone

From peaceful scenic floats to splash-filled whitewater, our guided rafting trips are designed for every comfort level and kind of crew. Explore your options, choose your pace, and get ready for an unforgettable day on the river.

Lazy Bend Scenic Float

A calm, easygoing float designed for soaking up the scenery and enjoying the river at a relaxed pace. Perfect for families, first-timers, and anyone looking for a peaceful day on the water.

Splash & Smile Adventure Run

A lively stretch of river with playful rapids and plenty of splash-worthy moments. This trip strikes the perfect balance between excitement and approachability for adventurous groups and confident beginners.

Whitewater Rush Expedition

For those craving bigger rapids and nonstop energy, this high-adrenaline run delivers thrilling whitewater and unforgettable action. Best suited for adventurous paddlers ready to take on the river’s wilder side.
