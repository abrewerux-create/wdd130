The Happy Paddle Raft Co.

Our motto isn’t just a catchy phrase—it’s how we run every trip. We believe the best river adventures feel equal parts safe, welcoming, and unforgettable, no matter your age or experience level. From the first paddle stroke to the final high-five, our guides are there to encourage you, keep things smooth, and make sure you’re having the time of your life. Because at the end of the day, the goal is simple: leave the river happier than you came

History

The Happy Paddle Raft Co. started with one raft, one beat-up truck, and a simple idea: river days should feel like the best day of summer.

Back in 2014, two best friends (and lifelong outdoors lovers) began taking friends and family down their favorite stretch of river every weekend. Word spread fast—because the trips weren’t just about the rapids… they were about the laughter, the music, the snacks, the goofy group photos, and the guides who made everyone feel confident and safe.

By 2017, those weekend float trips turned into a real business, built on a reputation for friendly guides, family-first fun, and the kind of river memories people talk about for years.

Today, The Happy Paddle Raft Co. is proud to be a local favorite—still led by the same mission that started it all: make every guest feel like part of the crew, and every trip feel like pure joy.
